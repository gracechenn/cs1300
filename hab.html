Identity Design for Hack@Brown 2022

Identity Design, 3D Animation

Introduction

Hack@Brown is Brown University's annual hackathon that welcomes high schoolers and college students around the world. Each year, we choose a playful theme like "Afterschool" or "Greenhouse" to encourage students of all backgrounds to join.

In 2022, I led the design team to create a brand identity for Brown's annual Hackathon ‚Äì including art direction, web design, illustrations, and social media.

Each year, we choose a playful theme to encourage students of all backgrounds to join. "Greenhouse" represents a safe space where students can grow and it hopes to center the event around eco-friendly hacks.

Not only do we encourage hackers to learn, but everyone on our design team joins with no prior knowledge of how to 3D model.

We used vibrant colors and simplified 3D illustrations to create an immersive website homepage.
